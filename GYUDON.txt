regexp

^((-?[a-z0-9-]+)[:+,])*(-?[a-z0-9-]+)$

valid characters a-z, 0-9 and - for tags

order of operations

: logical and (infix)
+ logical and (infix)
, logical or (infix)
- logical not (prefix)

examples

downloads:ringtones:cool+free

software:unix,linux,bsd

news:-lgbtq

games:mods:counter-strike:maps+dm
